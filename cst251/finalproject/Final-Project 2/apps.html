Apps For Students



Evernote

Evernote is a comprehensive note-taking and organization app that allows users to capture, store, and manage information across various formats and devices, with features such as web clipping, handwriting recognition, and cross-platform integration.



Remember the Milk

Remember the Milk is a task management app that helps users keep track of their to-do lists, reminders, and deadlines across multiple devices.



ToDoList

Todoist is a versatile and intuitive task management app that enables users to create, track, and collaborate on to-do lists and projects, while offering advanced features like natural language input and productivity tracking.
